About Inter-Circle Symposium

  • I.C.S started as a collaborative effort to bring together freelancers in the software field to create an efficient environment to work on big projects as a team. Since then, we have come a long way to bring ideas to life.



Our vision

  • We are aiming to create one of the largest networks of business developers in all fields worldwide.



Our mission

  • Our mission is to continually grow our network by adding fellow members in compliance with our goals.
